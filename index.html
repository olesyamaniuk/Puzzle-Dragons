Game Description

Puzzle & Dragons brings together classic match-3 puzzle mechanics with immersive RPG elements and deep monster collecting. Build your dream team from hundreds of unique monsters, power them up, and unleash devastating attacks by matching colored orbs.
With constantly updated content, challenging dungeons, and team-building strategy, Puzzle & Dragons offers endless excitement and tactical gameplay.

How to Play

Match 3+ orbs of the same color to launch attacks.
Build a team of monsters with complementary skills.
Defeat enemies in dungeons and earn rewards.
Evolve and fuse monsters to make them stronger.
Take on special events and limited-time challenges.

FAQ

  • Is Puzzle & Dragons free to play?
    Yes, with optional in-game purchases.
  • Can I play offline?
    You need an internet connection to access most features.
  • How do I evolve monsters?
    Collect evolution materials and use them in the Monster Box.
  • What are collab events?
    Special themed events with exclusive dungeons and monsters.
  • Is there multiplayer?
    Yes! You can team up with friends in multiplayer dungeons.
